# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Uni-Nest Uganda ("Uni-Nest", "we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our websites and applications, including student, owner, and admin portals (collectively, the "Services").

By using the Services, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following categories:

### 1.1 Information you provide directly

- Account data: first name, last name, email, password (managed by authentication provider), role.
- Profile and booking data: phone number, course, move-in date, next of kin, sponsor, origin, medical history, and related booking details.
- Property/listing data (owners/admins): property descriptions, pricing, room details, amenities, and uploaded image URLs.
- Communications and feedback: messages, support requests, reviews, and ratings.
- Payment-related data: transaction details and status metadata needed to process and reconcile payments.

### 1.2 Information collected automatically

- Device and usage data: browser type, operating system, IP address, pages viewed, feature usage, timestamps, and referral URLs.
- Technical logs: error logs, diagnostics, and security logs.

### 1.3 Information from third parties

- Authentication/session data from our identity provider.
- Payment status and transaction references from payment service providers.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use personal information to:

- Create and manage user accounts.
- Match students with available hostels and room types.
- Process bookings, payments, notifications, and confirmations.
- Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access.
- Enforce platform rules and role-based authorization.
- Improve platform features, performance, and reliability.
- Comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests.

## 3. Legal Bases (where applicable)

Depending on your location, we process personal data based on one or more of the following:

- Performance of a contract (providing Services you request).
- Legitimate interests (security, fraud prevention, service improvement).
- Legal obligations.
- Your consent (for specific optional processing activities).

## 4. Role-Based Access and Authorization

We implement access controls to limit data access by role (for example: student, hostel owner, super admin). Access is enforced by application controls and database security policies, including row-level access controls where applicable.

## 5. Sharing of Information

We may share information:

- With service providers that help us operate the Services (hosting, authentication, database, analytics, payments).
- Between relevant platform participants as needed to complete bookings (for example, student and property owner details related to a booking).
- With legal authorities when required by law, regulation, court order, or to protect rights and safety.
- In connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets.

We do not sell personal information.

## 6. Data Retention

We keep personal information for as long as needed to:

- Provide and maintain the Services.
- Meet legal, tax, accounting, and compliance requirements.
- Resolve disputes and enforce agreements.

Retention periods may differ by data category and legal requirement.

## 7. Data Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

## 8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your own. Where required, we implement reasonable safeguards for cross-border transfers.

## 9. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

- Access personal information we hold about you.
- Correct inaccurate personal information.
- Request deletion of personal information.
- Object to or restrict certain processing.
- Request data portability.

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below. We may need to verify your identity before responding.
